Projects

Rotting Research currently has two projects:

The first project is Rotting Research itself. Rotting Research was created to help researchers and scholars check their publications for symptoms of link rot and educate them on ways to mitigate the problem.

The second project is linkrot. Linkrot is a Python library that allows us to mine PDFs for links, track the types of links, and check whether they are active. Linkrot is the launchpad for Rotting Research.
